Home Services Digital marketing

1. Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

  1. On-page optimization: Keyword research, content optimization, meta tag optimization, and URL structuring.
  2. Off-page optimization: Link building, social bookmarking, online directory submissions, and guest blogging.
  3. Technical SEO: Website speed optimization, mobile responsiveness, XML sitemap creation, and robots.txt management.
  4. SEO audits and analysis to identify opportunities for improvement and monitor keyword rankings.

2. Pay-Per-Click Advertising (PPC)

  1. Google Ads (formerly AdWords) and Bing Ads campaign setup and management.
  2. Keyword research, ad copy creation, bid management, and landing page optimization.
  3. Conversion tracking and performance measurement through analytics tools.
  4. A/B testing of ads and landing pages for improved ad performance and conversion rates.

3. Social Media Marketing

  1. Creation and management of social media profiles on platforms like Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, and YouTube.
  2. Content creation and scheduling, including text, images, videos, and infographics.
  3. Audience targeting and engagement through organic and paid social media campaigns.
  4. Social media analytics and reporting to measure reach, engagement, and conversion rates.

4. Content Marketing

  1. Content strategy development to attract and engage target audiences.
  2. Creation of high-quality, relevant, and optimized content such as blog posts, articles, whitepapers, and case studies.
  3. Content distribution through various channels, including website/blog, social media, and email marketing.
  4. Content performance analysis and optimization based on user engagement and conversion metrics.

5. Email Marketing

  1. Creation and management of email marketing campaigns, including newsletter design, content creation, and subscriber segmentation.
  2. Integration with email marketing software for automated campaigns, personalization, and tracking.
  3. A/B testing of email subject lines, content, and call-to-action buttons.
  4. Performance analysis through metrics like open rates, click-through rates, and conversion rates.

6. Online Reputation Management (ORM)

  1. Monitoring and managing online brand reputation and customer reviews.
  2. Responding to customer feedback, addressing complaints, and resolving issues.
  3. Building positive brand sentiment through proactive engagement and content promotion.
  4. Implementing strategies to mitigate the impact of negative online mentions or reviews.

7. Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)

  1. Analysis and optimization of website or landing page elements to improve conversion rates.
  2. User experience (UX) improvements, such as clear call-to-action buttons, intuitive navigation, and mobile responsiveness.
  3. A/B testing of page layouts, headlines, forms, and other elements to identify the most effective variations.
  4. Conversion tracking setup and analysis to measure and optimize key conversion points.

8. Analytics and Reporting

  1. Implementation and configuration of web analytics tools like Google Analytics or Adobe Analytics.
  2. Customized reporting and analysis of website traffic, user behavior, and conversion funnels.
  3. Performance tracking of digital marketing campaigns, channels, and key performance indicators (KPIs).
  4. Data-driven insights and recommendations to optimize marketing strategies and investment.

Remember, these details can vary depending on the specific digital marketing service provider. It's essential to discuss your requirements with the service provider to ensure they can meet your business goals and deliver the desired digital marketing solutions.
