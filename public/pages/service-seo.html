01.

SEO Audit and Analysis

  1. Comprehensive website analysis to identify technical issues, on-page optimization opportunities, and areas for improvement.
  2. Keyword research and analysis to determine relevant keywords and phrases for targeting.
  3. Competitor analysis to understand their SEO strategies and identify areas of competitive advantage.
  4. Identification of website performance metrics, including organic traffic, rankings, and user engagement.
02.

On-Page Optimization

  1. Keyword optimization of website content, including headings, meta tags, URLs, and image alt tags.
  2. Content optimization to ensure relevance, readability, and user engagement.
  3. Site structure optimization, including navigation, internal linking, and XML sitemap creation.
  4. Optimization of page load speed, mobile responsiveness, and usability for enhanced user experience.
03.

Off-Page Optimization

  1. Link building strategies to increase the number and quality of backlinks to the website
  2. Guest blogging, outreach, and content promotion to generate high-quality backlinks from authoritative websites
  3. Social media signals and social bookmarking to enhance website visibility and reach
  4. Online directory submissions and local SEO optimization for location-based businesses
04.

Technical SEO

  1. Website crawlability and indexability analysis to ensure search engines can access and understand the website's content
  2. Website speed optimization to improve user experience and search engine rankings.
  3. Mobile optimization to ensure the website is mobile-friendly and meets mobile search requirements.
  4. Schema markup implementation to enhance search engine understanding of structured data on the website.
05.

Content Creation and Optimization

  1. Creation of high-quality, relevant, and optimized content for website pages, blog posts, articles, and other resources.
  2. Keyword integration and optimization within content to improve visibility for target search terms.
  3. Content strategy development to align with target audience needs and search intent.
  4. Optimization of multimedia content such as images and videos for search engines.
06.

Local SEO

  1. Local keyword research and optimization for businesses targeting specific geographic locations.
  2. Google My Business optimization to enhance local business visibility on Google Maps and local search results.
  3. Online reviews management and reputation management for local businesses.
  4. Local citation building and NAP (Name, Address, Phone number) consistency across directories and listings.
07.

SEO Reporting and Analysis

  1. Regular monitoring and reporting of website performance metrics, including organic traffic, keyword rankings, and conversions.
  2. Analysis of user behavior, click-through rates, and bounce rates to identify areas of improvement.
  3. Identification of trends, opportunities, and recommendations for ongoing SEO optimization.
  4. Customized reports and dashboards to track progress and measure ROI.
08.

SEO Consultation and Strategy

  1. Consultation services to understand business goals and develop an effective SEO strategy
  2. Guidance on best practices, industry trends, and algorithm updates
  3. Assistance with website migrations, domain changes, or other SEO-sensitive activities.
  4. Ongoing SEO support, guidance, and collaboration with internal teams.
